# Motorway Case Study: incident.io

## Customer Information
**Company:** Motorway
**Industry:** E-commerce / Used Car Marketplace
**Location:** UK
**Scale:** 7,500+ dealers, 2 cars sold per minute, 500,000+ sales facilitated

## Leadership
- **Matt Sleeman** - VP of Engineering

## Challenge
Before implementing incident.io, Bold Commerce faced several workflow inefficiencies:
- Incident coordination occurred through random, disparate Slack channels
- A "911 channel" required manual setup of incident channels, even for non-operational issues
- No standardized process for managing time-sensitive projects and collaborative work
- Difficulty tracking and documenting critical events for compliance and post-analysis

## Solution
Motorway adopted incident.io to replace their fragmented incident management approach using OpsGenie and Jira.

### Why They Chose incident.io
Matt Sleeman, VP of Engineering, explained: *"If you're going to change someone's muscle memory, it needs to be a really substantial improvement."* incident.io offered:
- End-to-end incident lifecycle management (alerting to resolution)
- Cultural alignment with continuous improvement focus
- Significant operational uplift over fragmented approach
- Team expertise in incident management practices

### Implementation
- **Timeline:** 2-week full deployment
- **Proof of concept:** 2 days
- **Strategy:** Platform team led setup and became internal advocates
- **Key support:** Shared Slack channel with incident.io team; test incident feature for safe exploration

### Critical Feature: Scribe (AI Documentation)
- Automated stakeholder updates during incidents
- Eliminated context-switching between platforms
- Captured post-mortem details automatically
- Enabled remote incident monitoring without disrupting response teams

## Results & Outcomes

### Quantified Improvements
✅ **60% improvement** in proactive issue detection (internal observability vs. customer reports)
✅ **Eliminated 5-minute administrative setup** process through automated workflows
✅ **Reduced reactive incident time** for engineers, increasing development velocity

### Operational Changes
- **Better cross-functional collaboration:** Customer success teams now raise triage tickets, preventing dismissal of issues
- **Data-driven culture:** Bi-weekly operational excellence meetings now feature incident metrics and analysis
- **Cultural shift:** Incidents reframed from negative to learning opportunities

## Key Quotes

**Matt Sleeman, VP of Engineering:**
> "Telling people: 'You have to read the P1 incident process, learn it and be able to do it at 3:00 AM' isn't great."

> "The Scribe feature alone is worth it. We can focus on getting the fix done, just following along remotely."

> "We've drastically reduced reactive incident time, which translated into increased velocity and supports our innovation value."

## Business Impact
For a marketplace handling emotionally and financially significant transactions (cars often represent people's second-largest asset), reliable incident management directly supports customer trust, platform stability, and engineering team capacity for innovation.
