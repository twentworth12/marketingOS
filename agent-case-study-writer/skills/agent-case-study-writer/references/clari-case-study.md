# Clari Case Study: Global Incident Management with incident.io

## Customer
**Clari** - A revenue platform company

## Industry
Enterprise SaaS / Revenue Operations

## Key Stakeholders
- **Balaji Narayanan** - Senior Director of Engineering
- **Kurt Andersen** - Software Architect

## Challenges

### Manual and Inconsistent Processes
Clari's incident response workflow had become unmanageable as the organization grew. Processes existed in Confluence and Google Docs but weren't consistently executed across teams.

### Process Variability
Different incident commanders approached responses differently, creating unpredictable outcomes. As Kurt noted, the process was heavily influenced by individual preferences rather than standardized practices.

### Context Sharing Gaps
New responders joining incidents couldn't quickly get up to speed. Documentation was sparse, and context sharing relied on ad hoc Zoom calls and individual initiative.

### Lack of Global Consistency
With operations across multiple regions, maintaining uniform incident management practices proved nearly impossible.

## Solution
Clari implemented **incident.io**, a dedicated incident response platform that provided:

- Intuitive incident declaration and coordination
- Automated workflows and repetitive processes
- Enhanced visibility for managers and executives
- Data visualization for post-incident learning
- Standardized processes across all teams and regions

## Results and Outcomes

### Unified Global Program
Successfully deployed incident management across North America, India, and Poland with consistent processes.

### Standardized Declaration Process
"The biggest advantage we have right now is that there's only one way to declare an incident" - achieved uniformity across all teams.

### Improved Accessibility
New incident commanders can execute responses without extensive context, reducing barriers to participation.

### Expanded Use Cases
Beyond customer incidents, the platform now manages security incidents and maintenance events using the same framework.

### Rapid Support Response
The incident.io team provides fixes in days rather than weeks, demonstrating strong partnership commitment.

## Key Metrics and Benefits

- Single, standardized incident declaration process
- Consistent incident tracking and evaluation methodology
- Support turnaround measured in hours to days
- Seamless onboarding of new incident responders
