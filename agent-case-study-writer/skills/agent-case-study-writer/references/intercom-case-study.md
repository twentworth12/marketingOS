# Intercom Case Study: incident.io Migration

## Customer
**Intercom** - A SaaS company specializing in personalized customer messaging and support solutions.

## Industry
SaaS / Customer Engagement Platform

## Challenge
Intercom's incident management process was fragmented and manual across multiple disconnected tools (Slack, PagerDuty, GitHub). Key issues included:

- Static Slack channels that couldn't handle simultaneous incidents
- Minor incidents frequently went undocumented, reducing organizational learning
- Inconsistent processes requiring engineers and incident commanders to improvise responses
- Customer support teams manually chasing engineers for updates, causing delays
- Incomplete follow-ups and unresolved documentation issues
- Difficulty analyzing trends due to incident data scattered across platforms

> "We had two Slack channels. You had to hope there weren't multiple incidents happening simultaneously. Overall, not a great experience." - *Intercom team member*

## Solution
Intercom adopted incident.io as an end-to-end incident management platform, consolidating:

- **On-call management** (replacing PagerDuty)
- **Incident response workflows** (replacing manual, in-house processes)
- **Status pages** (replacing Atlassian Status Page)

Key features that drove adoption:
- Deep Slack integration with automated incident channel creation
- Automated incident summaries and status updates
- Regional status page capabilities
- Centralized dashboard for all incident data

## Implementation & Adoption
Migration completed in weeks with minimal disruption. The team ran incident.io alongside PagerDuty temporarily to validate reliability, then rapidly expanded adoption across engineering, customer support, and go-to-market teams.

> "The transition was incredibly smooth. Our engineers immediately preferred incident.io over PagerDuty, and adoption across the broader company quickly followed." - *Mark Gorman, Technical Program Manager*

> "Jumping between multiple tools during high-pressure incidents was challenging. Now everything is centralized in incident.io, simplifying incident response significantly." - *Brian Scanlan, Senior Principal Engineer*

## Results & Outcomes

- **Faster incident resolution**: Reduced mean time to resolution (MTTR) and minimized downtime
- **Enhanced visibility**: Comprehensive incident documentation enabling proactive improvements
- **Lower cognitive load**: AI-generated summaries and real-time highlights reduce manual review burden
- **Improved customer trust**: Integrated regional status pages provide timely, accurate customer communication
- **Reduced friction**: Easier team mobilization during high-pressure situations
- **Organizational culture shift**: Broader engagement in incident management fostering collaborative problem-solving and continuous learning

> "incident.io's automated summaries and real-time highlights have dramatically reduced cognitive overhead. Engineers and executives quickly understand incidents without manual intervention, vastly improving our overall experience." - *Ryan Sherlock, Director of Engineering*
