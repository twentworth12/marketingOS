# incident.io Status Page Case Study: RD Station

## Customer Information
- **Company:** RD Station
- **Industry:** SaaS (Marketing, Sales, and Growth Management)
- **Company Size:** 700+ employees worldwide
- **Region:** Latin America (largest SaaS company in the region)

## The Challenge

RD Station struggled with their existing status page solution due to:

- **Poor User Experience:** The interface lacked intuitiveness and user-friendliness
- **Severe Limitations:** Restricted ability to share routine communications like alert emails and maintenance notifications
- **Stagnant Product Development:** No clear product roadmap or evolution strategy
- **Communication Gaps:** Difficulty ensuring all customers received timely incident updates

> "We've had several problems with our status page's interface. Above all, it's not user-friendly."

The company needed a solution offering:
- Robust integrations with existing incident response tools
- Straightforward UI for quick incident comprehension
- Responsive product team committed to customer feedback

## The Solution

RD Station adopted **incident.io's Status Pages product**, which integrated seamlessly into their existing incident response workflows. The platform consolidated their entire incident management process from declaration through post-mortem generation.

## Results & Outcomes

- **Improved Customer Communications:** Enhanced ability to inform customers during critical incidents
- **Streamlined Information Visualization:** Clearer consolidation of incident details
- **Faster Workflows:** SSO and tool integration enabled rapid, confident operations
- **Early Product Partnership:** Actively participated in the Status Pages design process

> "We've improved communication with our customers during critical moments and streamlined incident information."

> "We've been a partner of incident.io since the beginning...we've seen a lot of new features and a level of service we've never had."

## Key Takeaway

RD Station values the long-term partnership approach and incident.io's commitment to continuous innovation and customer collaboration.
