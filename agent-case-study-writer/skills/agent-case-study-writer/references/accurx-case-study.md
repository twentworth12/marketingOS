# Accurx Case Study: Incident.io Implementation

## Customer
**Accurx** - Healthcare software startup providing communication tools for GP practices across the UK

## Industry
Healthcare Technology / Primary Care Services

## Challenge

Accurx faced critical incident management obstacles:

- **Compliance Requirements**: Stringent NHS contractual obligations mandating incident escalation and resolution within agreed timeframes
- **Visibility Gaps**: Incidents managed inconsistently through Slack (minor bugs in threads, major outages in temporary channels), causing information loss and poor tracking
- **Scalability Issues**: As the company grew, communication between teams (clinical, engineering, comms, commercial) became increasingly chaotic
- **Data Sensitivity**: Need to handle healthcare data securely while demonstrating reliable incident response processes

## Solution

Adopted **incident.io** to centralize and standardize incident management:

- Consolidated all incident tracking in Slack with structured workflows
- Customized NHS terminology and incident categorization
- Automated prompts based on severity levels and incident types
- Created consistent postmortem documentation templates
- Enabled cross-team collaboration in hybrid/remote environments

## Results & Outcomes

### Time Savings
> "Saves us 1-2 hours per incident" through automated documentation, stakeholder communication, and reporting

### Improved Visibility
> "Get a clear picture of what's going on, at individual incident and overall levels…kept really strong eye on product quality"

### Process Consistency
Automated prompts enforce standardized responses:
> "You've set this to severity two, you need to call X within this time"

### NHS Compliance
Automatic postmortem generation enabled auditors to verify exact timelines, responsible parties, and discussions for regulatory review.

### Near-Miss Tracking
Team leverages system to log preventive incidents, reinforcing accountability and organizational learning.

## Key Metrics
- Incidents raised several times weekly for tracking
- Faster identification of recurring problems
- Positive NHS audit feedback directly attributed to incident.io implementation
- Measurable improvement in incident response times
