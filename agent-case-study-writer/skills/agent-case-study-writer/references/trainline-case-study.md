# Trainline Case Study: Incident Management with incident.io

## Customer
**Trainline** - A travel company managing incidents across platform operations and multiple teams

## Industry
Travel & Transportation Technology

## Leadership
- **Dan Cook** - Reliability and Operations Manager

## Challenge

Trainline faced several critical pain points with their incident management process:

- **Channel Fragmentation**: Three separate Slack channels (triage, P1, P2) created confusion when multiple incidents occurred simultaneously, forcing teams to branch into additional channels
- **Information Overload**: Engineers returning to work faced hundreds of messages they had to parse through, losing context about active incidents
- **Manual Communication Process**: Stakeholder updates required accessing separate tools and manually forwarding information, creating delays during critical moments
- **Lack of Clarity**: Non-technical executives couldn't understand incident context within Slack channels, leading to confusion about customer impact
- **Scalability Issues**: The existing process became increasingly inefficient as the organization grew

## Solution

Trainline adopted **incident.io**, which provided:

- Centralized incident management with dedicated channels
- Automated communication workflows for external notifications
- Clear visibility into incident ownership and affected services
- Support for custom incident types
- Private channel functionality for sensitive incidents

## Results & Outcomes

### Key Achievements

- **Expanded Incident Types**: Created a new P3 incident category for lower-impact issues previously undermanaged
- **Improved Visibility**: Company-wide access to incident channels ensuring transparency across technical and business teams
- **Workflow Automation**: Eliminated manual communication overhead for third-party notifications
- **Time Savings**: Reduced incident response administrative tasks by approximately 20 minutes per incident
- **Broader Adoption**: Extended incident.io beyond platform incidents to security incident management

## Customer Quotes

**Dan Cook, Reliability and Operations Manager:**

> "The role of an incident manager is relatively simple: it's to coordinate people to resolve an incident and ensure stakeholder management."

He also noted the immediate impact:

> "Within running a couple of incidents through the platform, we could see the benefits."

## Impact Statement

Dan expressed confidence in the solution's long-term value: Trainline views incident.io as integral to supporting continued growth and plans to expand its use across additional organizational functions.
