# SumUp Case Study: Incident Response Excellence with incident.io

## Customer
**SumUp** - A global fintech company operating in over 25 countries, providing point-of-sale (POS) solutions for businesses of all sizes.

## Industry
Financial Technology / Payment Processing

## Leadership
- **Adrián Moreno Peña** - VP of Engineering

## Challenge
Before implementing incident.io, SumUp faced several critical obstacles:

- **Manual processes**: An in-house incident response bot required extensive manual overhead, including relying on sticky notes for status page updates
- **Context switching**: Responders had to toggle between multiple tools (response bot and separate status page), causing distractions during critical incidents
- **Cognitive overload**: Disparate systems created unnecessary friction in incident management workflows
- **Scalability concerns**: Manual processes became increasingly unmanageable as the company grew

## Solution
SumUp adopted **incident.io**, consolidating incident response and communications into a unified platform that includes:

- Integrated Status Page functionality (both internal and external)
- AI-powered incident summaries
- Streamlined response workflows
- Single-tool incident declaration and management

## Results & Outcomes

**Operational Improvements:**
- Eliminated context switching between multiple tools
- Reduced cognitive overload during incidents
- Faster incident response times through process automation
- Seamless communication with both customers and internal stakeholders

**Key Benefits:**
- "AI summaries are saving us hours of work a month" - addressing the costly back-and-forth between teams
- Internal status pages enable transparency across operations, customer success, and compliance teams
- Automated decision-making reduces mental burden during high-stress situations
