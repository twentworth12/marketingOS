# Thrive Learning: incident.io Case Study

## Customer Profile
**Company:** Thrive Learning
**Industry:** EdTech / Learning Management Systems (LMS)
**Scale:** 400+ organizations, millions of users worldwide
**Founded:** Five years prior to case study

## Leadership
- **Leigh Darlow** - Head of Live Service

## Challenges

Thrive Learning faced operational friction in their incident response workflow:

- **Tool Fragmentation:** Relied on disconnected systems (PagerDuty, chat platform, document storage, New Relic, Jira)
- **Slow Response:** Manual setup of communication channels and documentation delayed incident resolution
- **Administrative Burden:** Incident managers manually tracked and managed follow-up action items
- **Limited Learning:** Siloed data prevented identification of patterns and recurring issues

**Stakeholder Context:**
> "Before incident.io, we had a manual process. Spinning up incidents was inefficient, which slowed us from resolving the issue."
— Leigh Darlow, Head of Live Service

## Solution Requirements

Future sought:
- Rapid implementation capability
- Native integrations with existing tools (New Relic, Jira)
- Automated workflows and AI-powered incident summaries
- Superior cost-effectiveness compared to previous solution

## Implementation Timeline

**Total Duration:** One month

- **Proof of Concept (2 weeks):** Configured incident.io to match and enhance existing workflows
- **Parallel Operation (2 weeks):** Ran incident.io alongside legacy tools to validate reliability before full transition

## Results & Outcomes

✅ **Accelerated Mobilization:** Automated incident channels and alerting substantially reduced response times

✅ **Administrative Efficiency:** Automated status updates and timeline generation eliminated manual documentation work

✅ **Preventive Culture Shift:** Lower incident declaration barriers encouraged earlier problem reporting before customer impact

✅ **Cross-functional Collaboration:** Engineering, QA, DevOps, and Support teams actively participated in incidents

✅ **Pattern Recognition:** Centralized incident data enabled identification and prevention of recurring issues

**Leadership Perspective:**
> "incident.io made it so easy to declare incidents that more people proactively raise them. We're catching problems before they become customer-impacting issues."
— Leigh Darlow
