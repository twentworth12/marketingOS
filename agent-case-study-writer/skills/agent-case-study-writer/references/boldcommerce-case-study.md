# Bold Commerce Case Study: Reimagining Incident Management

## Customer Name
**Bold Commerce**

## Industry
E-commerce / Payment Processing

## Company Overview
Bold Commerce serves as a checkout solution for global companies, with operations spread across the North American region.

## Leadership
- **Craig Kinloch-Melia** - Head of Technology
- **Eric** - Co-founder

## Challenge
Before implementing incident.io, Bold Commerce faced several workflow inefficiencies:
- Incident coordination occurred through random, disparate Slack channels
- A "911 channel" required manual setup of incident channels, even for non-operational issues
- No standardized process for managing time-sensitive projects and collaborative work
- Difficulty tracking and documenting critical events for compliance and post-analysis

## Solution
Bold Commerce adopted **incident.io** as their centralized workflow management platform, initially using the open-source version developed by Chris Evans at Monzo Bank before transitioning to the commercial offering.

### Key Features Leveraged
- **Workflows**: Customizable incident types and automated nudges
- **Private Incident Channels**: For security incidents with legal documentation requirements
- **Automated Integrations**: PagerDuty connections for executive notifications
- **Timeline Recording**: Automated capture of incident details and summaries
- **Post-Mortem Documentation**: Built-in follow-up tracking and closure verification

## Use Cases Beyond Traditional Incident Response

### 1. Security Incidents
"If there's anything that's a data issue, we have a default private incident that's generated, and then we use that channel to record everything about the incident."

### 2. Tiger Teams
Rapid response to urgent feature requests and non-incident problems requiring cross-functional collaboration.

### 3. Black Friday & Cyber Monday
"We have set up a Workflow to say, if you're working on Black Friday and create an incident, just ping it straight over to PagerDuty and just page both of us."

### 4. Product Launches
Centralized coordination during go-live events with automated documentation and follow-up tracking.

### 5. Planned Maintenance
Proactive communication about maintenance windows to prevent confusion about system issues.

## Results & Outcomes

### Operational Improvements
- **Simplified Adoption**: Low training requirements due to intuitive design
- **Faster Response Times**: Automated alert routing eliminates coordination delays
- **Better Documentation**: AI-powered features automatically capture critical incident details
- **Improved Visibility**: Executive awareness without constant channel monitoring

### Key Quotes

**Craig Kinloch-Melia, Head of Technology:**

> "Just because it's called incident.io doesn't mean it's just for incidents. It's actually a workflow tool for us at this point."

> "The documentation and training we have on incident.io is much less than anything we have with other tooling because it's intuitive."

> "It helps us be very responsive as a company now that we're remote, spread across the North American region."

## Notable Insights
- Bold Commerce has been using incident.io (or its predecessor) since before the commercial product launch
- The platform's flexibility allowed expansion from incident response to broader workflow management
- The solution addresses the reality that critical work extends far beyond emergency responses
