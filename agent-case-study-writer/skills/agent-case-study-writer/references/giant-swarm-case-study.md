# Giant Swarm Case Study: Incident Management with incident.io

## Customer Profile
**Company:** Giant Swarm
**Industry:** Cloud-native infrastructure / Kubernetes platform
**Company Size:** 80 employees, fully remote
**Service:** Fully managed, open source Kubernetes platform for enterprise digital transformation

## Challenge
Giant Swarm struggled with incident response consistency as their team expanded. Their previous approach relied on OpsGenie for alerting and Slack for coordination, but lacked formal structure and depended on a few knowledgeable individuals to guide the process.

Key pain points included:
- Ineffective onboarding of new on-call engineers in a distributed environment
- High-pressure decision-making without process clarity
- Difficulty scaling incident management procedures

> "Telling people: 'You have to read the P1 incident process, learn it and be able to do it at 3:00 AM' isn't great."

## Solution
Implemented incident.io to standardize and automate their incident response workflows.

## Results & Outcomes

**Process Standardization**
- Encoded incident procedures, reducing cognitive load during critical events
- Created a scalable model supporting company growth
- Estimated 3-hour reduction in onboarding time per new on-caller

**Customer Success Integration**
- Improved incident visibility for non-engineering teams
- Faster root cause analysis delivery to customers

**Post-Incident Improvements**
- Dramatically faster RCA generation and insights collection
- Improved follow-up capture from 40% to 90%

## Key Metrics
- RCA generation time: "Over an hour" → "Several minutes"
- Follow-up capture rate: 40% → 90%
- Onboarding savings: "At least 3 hours per new oncaller"
- Insight generation: "Minutes instead of hours" spent on spreadsheets
