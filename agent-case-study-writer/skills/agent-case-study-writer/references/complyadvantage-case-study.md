# incident.io Case Study: ComplyAdvantage

## Customer
**ComplyAdvantage** - Leader in AI-driven financial crime risk detection data and insights

## Industry
Financial Crime Compliance & Risk Detection

## Challenge
ComplyAdvantage manages critical, time-sensitive information requiring systems to run reliably with rapid incident mitigation. Their previous approach faced scalability issues:

- Manual incident management using Jira and Slack proved unscalable as the team grew
- Lack of standardized decision-making process for prioritization and next steps
- Fragmented and inconsistent incident response procedures

> "We previously had an incident management process that wasn't built to scale"

## Requirements
ComplyAdvantage sought:
- A scalable process capable of growing with the organization
- Streamlined incident management with reduced noise
- Standardized incident response procedures

## Solution
incident.io automated incident workflows with:

- **Automation**: Reduced manual steps from 15 to 2-3 per incident
- **Decision flows**: Standardized prioritization and management approaches
- **Built-in safeguards**: Nudges and prompts prevent oversights during high-stress incidents
- **Centralized communication**: Unified information access for all stakeholders

## Results & Outcomes

Engineers now dedicate less time managing incidents and more time developing products. Cross-functional teams—including security, legal, engineering, and executive leadership—receive synchronized information access during major incidents, eliminating communication gaps and stakeholder isolation.

> "incident.io has taken it down to two or three... let the automation do the rest"
