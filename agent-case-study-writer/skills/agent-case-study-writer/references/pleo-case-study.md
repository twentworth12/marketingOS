# Case Study: Pleo & incident.io

## Customer
**Pleo** - A spending solution for forward-thinking teams offering smart company cards across Europe

## Industry
Financial Services / FinTech (startup sector)

## Leadership
- **Gough** (Role not specified in source)

## Challenge
Pleo faced critical gaps in incident management:
- **Fragmented Communication**: Siloed, unclear communication across teams during simultaneous incidents with heavy context-chasing
- **Manual Documentation Burden**: Inefficient post-incident processes relying on hand-written postmortems
- **Growing Incident Backlog**: Rising incident volume causing engineering burnout and unclear prioritization

## Solution
Pleo implemented incident.io featuring:

### Automation & Communication
Workflows automation orchestrated response actions like channel population without manual intervention, enabling faster incident declarations via an intuitive interface.

### Intuitive Design
Streamlined declaration and response processes lowered the barrier for incident reporting, making it company-wide rather than engineer-only.

### Insights & Learning
Automated data collection replaced manual documentation, providing a unified source of truth for incident metrics.

## Key Quotes

*"It acts as the backbone to our processes and lets us automate many things. We ensure necessary response actions happen without human intervention."* — Gough

*"It's easier to spin up a Slack channel than parse information threads from our in-house tooling."* — Gough

*"The insights helped us identify areas to reduce mean time to recovery, which was huge."* — Gough

## Outcomes
- Reduced barrier to incident declaration
- Enhanced post-incident learning capabilities
- Improved mean time to recovery metrics
- Extended incident response culture beyond engineering
