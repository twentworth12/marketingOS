# Bud Financial Case Study

## Customer
**Bud Financial** - Financial services company

## Industry
Financial Services

## Leadership
- **Michael Cullum** - VP of Data and Engineering

## Challenge
Bud Financial lacked a structured, consolidated approach to incident response management. While they initially built an internal Slackbot to address the problem, it quickly revealed significant limitations including:

- Constraints around functionality expansion beyond basic incident declaration
- Difficulty scaling features and integrations (PagerDuty, GitHub, post-mortems)
- Ongoing maintenance burden and bug fixes requiring engineering resources
- Limited capabilities for creating flexible incident management processes

## Solution
Bud Financial replaced their custom Slackbot with **incident.io**, a dedicated incident management platform designed to handle incident declaration, management, and post-mortem workflows.

## Key Results & Outcomes

### Cost Impact
- Direct cost savings compared to maintaining internal tooling
- Reduced development overhead and maintenance expenses

### Operational Improvements
- More robust incident response processes from declaration through post-mortem
- Ability to implement flexible workflows without engineering involvement
- Improved incident management and response procedures

### Business Impact
- Better service delivery to clients
- Improved Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Enhanced net revenue retention and client satisfaction
- Positive impact on revenue and profitability

## Key Features Leveraged
- **Workflows**: Enables automated, flexible incident response processes
- **Catalog**: Provides powerful functionality for organizing incident information
- Custom alerts and notifications (e.g., SMS alerts for specific incidents)
