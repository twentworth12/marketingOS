# Isometric Case Study: incident.io

## Customer
**Isometric** - A carbon credit certification company that issues high-quality carbon credits backed by science and provides permanent audit trails for transparency.

## Industry
Climate Tech / Environmental Services

## Leadership
- **Ola Sitarska** - CTO

## Problem/Challenge

Ola Sitarska, CTO at Isometric, identified several critical gaps in incident management:

- **Fragmented Tools**: Teams needed to jump between disconnected systems that didn't communicate with one another
- **Communication Breakdowns**: A particularly challenging 12-hour incident revealed how difficult it was to keep communications streamlined across the entire company
- **On-Call Inefficiencies**: Manual override requests via Slack messages created friction and delays
- **Learning Barriers**: The bureaucracy around post-incident learning processes made it difficult to extract insights
- **Stagnant Market Solutions**: "What I've seen from others in this space is that those products just never change, evolve, or get better"

## Solution

Isometric became early adopters and design partners with incident.io, implementing:

- **End-to-End Platform**: A unified system managing incidents from alert through post-mortem
- **On-Call Integration**: Streamlined on-call scheduling with frictionless override capabilities
- **Insights Feature**: Data gathering and reporting tools for incident response analytics

## Results/Outcomes

- Eliminated Slack-based override requests in favor of integrated system
- Simplified monthly incident reporting process
- Reduced effort required to extract learning from incidents
- Created a cohesive incident response workflow
