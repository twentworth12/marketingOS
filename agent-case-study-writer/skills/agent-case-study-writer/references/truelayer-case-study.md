# TrueLayer Case Study: Reimagining Incident Management

## Customer
**TrueLayer** - European payments network and financial services company

## Industry
Financial Services / Payment Technology

## Leadership
- **Elizaveta Shevchenko** - Technical Support Lead

## Challenge
TrueLayer's incident management approach was limited to technical issues (API downtime, fraud claims, app failures). The organization lacked coordinated processes for non-technical incidents affecting reputation, regulatory compliance, and operations, resulting in fragmented workflows and slower response times across different teams.

## Solution
TrueLayer expanded their use of incident.io to encompass three additional incident categories beyond technical issues:

- **Operational incidents**: Financial accuracy errors and regulatory compliance issues
- **Reputational incidents**: Social media complaints and security-related public statements
- **Regulatory incidents**: SLA tracking and compliance reporting requirements

## Results & Outcomes

- Consolidated all incident types into a single platform for faster resolution
- Improved follow-up action tracking with clear ownership
- Enhanced leadership visibility and decision-making through centralized reporting
- Streamlined compliance and SLA metric reporting for regulatory requirements
- Faster response to reputational issues before escalation

## Notable Quotes

**Elizaveta Shevchenko, Technical Support Lead:**

> "We realized that we already have an app, incident.io, to log incidents. Why not just use it to keep everything in one place?"

> "Now, our C-levels and VPs are in the loop and are locked into one central system, and they're like, 'Okay, we know this is being handled.'"
