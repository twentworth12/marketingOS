# Picnic Case Study: incident.io Implementation

## Customer Information
- **Company Name:** Picnic
- **Location:** Amsterdam
- **Industry:** Online Grocery Delivery & E-Commerce
- **Business Model:** Hybrid digital and physical operations

## Company Overview
Picnic is a fast-growing online grocery delivery service combining cutting-edge digital technology with physical operations, including warehouses, delivery vehicle fleets, and sophisticated logistics planning.

## Challenge/Problem
Prior to implementing incident.io, Picnic faced several obstacles:

- **Fragmented tools:** On-call solution disconnected from incident management tool
- **Manual processes:** All incidents were created manually, lacking standardization
- **Team workarounds:** Individual teams developed custom solutions and Slack workflows due to tool limitations
- **Operational risk:** Unresolved incidents created cascading effects across supply chain and customer delivery

> "We did have an on-call solution, but this was disconnected from our incident tool" — Paulo, Tech Analyst

> "Picnic teams started to develop their own tools or their own Slack workflows because they weren't happy" — Paulo

## Solution
Picnic selected incident.io as their unified incident management platform to bridge technical and operational teams while supporting their hybrid business model.

### Implementation Approach
- Phased rollout starting with tech teams
- Added one team weekly over two quarters for complete tech adoption
- Created redirects from legacy tool directly to incident.io
- Expanded gradually to operational and business units

## Key Features Adopted
1. **Unified incident management** — Single platform for cross-functional collaboration
2. **Comprehensive visibility** — Complete overview of all incidents across the organization
3. **Automated post-incident workflows** — Streamlined follow-up processes and reporting

## Results & Outcomes

### Three Phases of Impact

**Phase 1: Faster, More Reliable Incident Handling**
- Improved response process efficiency
- Enhanced incident management confidence

> "incident.io is making it much easier to have the incidents solved" — Jenaro, Supervisor

**Phase 2: Reduction in Incident Frequency**
- Decreased number of incidents occurring
- Improved mitigation of incident impact
- Higher adoption rates and faster execution

**Phase 3: Structural Technical Resilience**
- Building preventative systems to eliminate customer-impacting incidents
- Moving toward zero-customer-impact incident response

> "We are now moving to a level where we are building structural technical resilience" — Daniel, CTO

## Key Stakeholder Quotes

> "Running tech in such a hybrid business is very complex. We were looking for a tool that allows us to operate in our live way." — Daniel, CTO

> "Having good cooperation between tech and business teams during incidents is vital for us" — Marc, Domain Lead for Operations

> "We fully deployed incident.io across all tech teams, operational teams, and first business teams. It fully replaced our internal tool and PagerDuty." — Paulo, Tech Analyst

## Broader Impact
- Replaced both internal incident tool and PagerDuty on-call platform
- Direct connection established between incident creation, escalations, and alerts
- Foundation built for reliable operations supporting Picnic's mission of simple, fun, and affordable grocery shopping
