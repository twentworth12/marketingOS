# incident.io Brand Point of View (Revised)

## Core beliefs

**Systems break. Always.** The question isn't if your production environment will fail - it's whether you'll be ready when it does. We believe the best incident management happens when the people who built the system are equipped to fix it, not when executives huddle around dashboards wondering what's happening.

**Engineers know best.** The person debugging the issue at 3 AM has more context than any manager reading a status page. We build tools for the people actually solving problems, not the people asking "when will it be fixed?"

**Speed beats process.** Perfect incident response documentation is worthless if your site is down for an extra hour while someone fills out forms. The best process is the one that gets out of your way and lets smart people do their jobs.

**Context is everything.** The difference between a 10-minute fix and a 4-hour debugging marathon usually comes down to having the right information at the right time. AI that investigates with the speed of a search engine and the knowledge of your most experienced SRE transforms how fast you can gather context, analyze patterns, and identify solutions.

**Trust your team.** Most incident management platforms assume your engineers are incompetent and need hand-holding. We assume they're capable professionals who need better tools, not more oversight.

**AI amplifies expertise.** The best AI doesn't replace engineers - it works alongside them as a knowledgeable teammate that never sleeps, never forgets, and always has context. AI that investigates, analyzes, and suggests solutions while humans focus on complex problem-solving and creative fixes.

## Mission and vision

**Mission**: Build the AI-powered incident management platform that engineers actually want to use - one that investigates alongside you, suggests fixes before you ask, and makes you more effective when things go wrong, instead of slowing you down with bureaucracy.

**Vision**: A world where AI handles the first 80% of incident response - gathering context, analyzing patterns, and suggesting solutions - so engineers can focus on what humans do best: complex problem-solving, creative fixes, and building resilient systems. Where incidents are resolved 5x faster with AI as your knowledgeable engineering teammate.

## Values

**Built by engineers, for engineers.** We don't just hire engineers to make marketing claims sound credible. Our product decisions come from people who've been on-call, debugged production at 2 AM, and felt the pain of terrible incident management tools.

**Honest about hard problems.** We don't pretend incident management is easy or that we've solved every edge case. When something's complex, we say so. When we don't have a feature, we're upfront about it. Trust comes from honesty, not marketing speak.

**Useful over impressive.** A simple feature that saves 20 minutes during every incident beats a fancy dashboard that looks good in demos. We optimize for the engineer trying to fix the problem, not the executive trying to explain it.

**Learn from failure.** Every outage, every incident, every mistake is data. The best teams turn problems into process improvements. The best tools make it easier to learn from what went wrong.
